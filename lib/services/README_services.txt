Services overview:
- auth_service.dart: Supabase Auth + users profile table.
- product_service.dart: products CRUD, search, category filter.
- cart_service.dart: carts and cart_items operations.
- coupon_service.dart: validate and manage coupons.
- order_service.dart: checkout and order history/admin order list.
- storage_service.dart: upload product images to storage bucket.
